IT'S SUPER GRASSY

It's Super Grassy is a shepherding adventure game. 

Grow your flock. 

Keep them moving to prevent overgrazing.

Guide them to avoid hazards.

Bring them inside at night to keep them safe.

Hire helping hands and paws.

Pass on your earnings to your descendants.

Solve challenging puzzles.

Explore new pastures and encounter new dangers!

Build settlements in distant lands.
